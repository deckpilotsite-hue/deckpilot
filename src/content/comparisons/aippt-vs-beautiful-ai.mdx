## Quick verdict

**AiPPT** generates decks in seconds from a prompt, document, or URL, with 200,000+ templates and AI image generation. Export (PPTX/PDF) requires Plus or Pro (~$9–12/mo). **Beautiful.ai** is template-first: Smart Slides auto-adjust layout as you type, keeping designs consistent and “board-ready.” It offers viewer analytics (Pro), PowerPoint import/export, and strong brand controls, but no permanent free tier—Pro $12/mo (annual), Team $40–50/user/mo. Choose AiPPT for fastest “content in → slides out” and maximum template variety; choose Beautiful.ai for design guardrails, engagement metrics, and a PowerPoint-centric workflow.

## Key differences

- **AI workflow:** AiPPT = one-click (or doc/URL) → full deck; strong generative side. Beautiful.ai = you build inside Smart Slide templates; AI assists content and design step-by-step, with prompt-to-deck/API as an add-on.
- **Design model:** AiPPT = large template set, drag-and-drop edit, brand kit; output quality can vary, some editing often needed. Beautiful.ai = Smart Slides enforce alignment and consistency; less freeform, fewer layout mistakes.
- **Inputs:** AiPPT = prompt, Word/PDF, or webpage URL. Beautiful.ai = prompt and content within the editor; no doc/URL ingestion in the same way.
- **Pricing:** AiPPT Free = 6 generations (2/day), no export; Plus ~$9/mo, Pro ~$12/mo. Beautiful.ai = 14-day trial (card required); Pro $12/mo (annual), Team $40–50/user/mo; no long-term free plan.
- **Analytics & export:** Beautiful.ai Pro includes viewer analytics (views, completion, time per slide) and editable PPTX/Google Slides export; PowerPoint add-in. AiPPT exports PPTX/PDF on paid plans; no built-in viewer analytics.

## When to choose AiPPT

- You want to turn existing material (notes, articles, documents, URLs) into slides as fast as possible.
- You value a huge template library and AI image generation (Nano Banana) over strict design automation.
- You’re solo or small team; viewer analytics and PowerPoint add-in are not must-haves.
- Best for: professionals, educators, and anyone who needs “content in → deck out” with minimal setup and is okay paying for export.

## When to choose Beautiful.ai

- You need “board/client-ready” consistency with minimal manual layout work; Smart Slides handle alignment.
- Viewer analytics matter for follow-up (who viewed, completion rate, time per slide).
- You work often in PowerPoint: import/export and add-in are central to your workflow.
- Best for: sales and marketing teams, and anyone who prioritizes design consistency and measurable sharing over maximum generative flexibility.

## Bottom line

**AiPPT** leads on speed and input flexibility (prompt/doc/URL → deck) and template volume; **Beautiful.ai** leads on design consistency, viewer analytics, and PowerPoint integration. Use AiPPT when the priority is fast slide generation from varied sources; use Beautiful.ai when the priority is polished, template-driven slides and engagement tracking.
