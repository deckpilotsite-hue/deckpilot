## Quick verdict

**Gamma** is “AI design partner” first: you give a prompt or document and get a full, themed deck in minutes using a card-based, web-native format. It’s fast and flexible for ideation; exports to PPTX/PDF can need cleanup. **Beautiful.ai** is template-first: Smart Slides auto-adjust layout as you type, keeping designs consistent and “board-ready.” It offers viewer analytics (Pro), PowerPoint import/export, and strong brand controls, but less generative “prompt-to-deck” depth. Choose Gamma for speed and a modern, scrollable output; choose Beautiful.ai for strict design guardrails and engagement tracking in a classic slide format.

## Key differences

- **Format & workflow:** Gamma = prompt or doc → AI generates full deck (outline + design); output is card-based, scrollable. Beautiful.ai = you build in Smart Slide templates that resize and align automatically; traditional 16:9 slides, step-by-step AI assistance rather than one-shot full generation.
- **Design control:** Gamma = theme-based layouts, limited freeform tweaking. Beautiful.ai = strong guardrails (Smart Slides), less creative freedom; layouts stay consistent by design.
- **AI depth:** Gamma = full deck from prompt, multiple AI models (text + images), optional Gamma Agent for refinement. Beautiful.ai = AI assists content and design within templates; prompt-to-deck and API exist but are more constrained.
- **Pricing:** Gamma Free = 400 credits, 10 cards; Plus ~$8–10/mo, Pro ~$20/mo (custom branding, API, collaboration). Beautiful.ai = Pro $12/mo (annual), Team $40–50/user/mo; 14-day trial, no permanent free tier for full features.
- **Analytics & export:** Beautiful.ai Pro includes viewer analytics (views, completion, slide-level time); Gamma Pro has analytics too. Both export PDF/PPTX; Beautiful.ai emphasizes editable PPTX and PowerPoint add-in; Gamma’s PPTX export can have format quirks.

## When to choose Gamma

- You want to go from prompt or document to a full deck in one flow, with minimal setup.
- You’re okay with a card-based, web-publishable format and theme-driven design.
- You value outline-first generation, optional Agent refinement, and (on Pro) API, custom branding, and analytics.
- Use cases: pitch decks, marketing one-pagers, internal docs, training slides where speed and a modern format matter more than strict 16:9 fidelity.

## When to choose Beautiful.ai

- You need “board/client-ready” consistency: Smart Slides keep alignment and branding in check with minimal manual layout work.
- Viewer analytics matter (who viewed, completion rate, time per slide) for sales or stakeholder follow-up.
- You work heavily in PowerPoint: import/export and add-in support are first-class.
- Use cases: sales decks, marketing collateral, and any scenario where design guardrails and analytics outweigh maximum generative flexibility.

## Bottom line

**Gamma** leads on AI-driven speed and breadth (prompt → full deck, multiple output types). **Beautiful.ai** leads on design consistency, viewer analytics, and PowerPoint-centric workflow. Pick Gamma for fastest drafts and a web-native feel; pick Beautiful.ai for polished, template-driven slides and measurable engagement.
