## Top alternatives to AiPPT

If AiPPT isn’t the right fit—for example you need real-time collaboration, a different export workflow, or stricter design guardrails—these tools offer similar or complementary features.

### When to consider switching

Choose **Gamma** if you want one AI-driven flow from prompt or document to a deck, real-time co-editing on paid plans, and a web-native, shareable format (with possible PPTX cleanup after export). Choose **Beautiful.ai** if you need traditional 16:9 slides, Smart Slides that auto-adjust as you type, viewer analytics (views, completion, slide-level time), and strong brand controls with high-fidelity PowerPoint export.
