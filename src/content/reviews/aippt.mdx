## Overview

AiPPT is a web-based AI presentation builder that generates slide decks from text prompts, uploaded documents (Word, PDF), or web URLs. It uses large-language models to turn your content into an outline and then populates slides with titles, bullets, and suggested images. The platform also includes the "Nano Banana" AI image engine for custom visuals and supports Google Drive import (Docs, Slides). You get a short free trial (6 AI generations, max 2 per day), but exporting final slides as PPTX or PDF requires a Plus or Pro subscription. Paid tiers start around $9–12/month with annual discounts; lifetime licenses are also available. AiPPT suits busy professionals and educators who want to turn notes or existing content into slides quickly, with a familiar slide-editor experience and drag-and-drop editing for fine-tuning.

## Key features

- **AI generation:** Full decks from a prompt, document, or URL; AI can also rewrite slide text, translate, rephrase bullets, and generate charts. Pro gets more image credits and faster generation; Nano Banana 2 (2026) improved image fidelity.
- **Templates and design:** Over 200,000 templates for business, education, and marketing; AI can suggest or swap designs. Custom fonts, colors, and logos with brand sync; drag-and-drop editing.
- **Export and integration:** PPTX, PDF, PNG/JPG export; layouts preserve in PowerPoint and Google Slides. No public API for automation as of 2026.

## Verdict

AiPPT is a solid choice when you need to go from topic or document to a presentable deck in minutes. Its strengths are speed, a very large template library, and flexible input sources. Quality can vary—some outputs are generic and need editing—and the free tier is limited (no export without paying). Best for founders, educators, and marketers who want fast first drafts and are okay subscribing for full export and daily limits. For highly bespoke designs or heavy multimedia, a traditional tool or another AI option may suit better.
