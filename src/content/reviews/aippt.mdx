## Overview

AiPPT is a web-based AI presentation builder that turns text prompts, documents (Word, PDF), or web URLs into slide decks. It uses language models to create an outline and fill slides with titles, bullets, and suggested images. The platform includes the **Nano Banana** AI image engine for custom visuals and supports Google Drive (Docs, Slides). A short free trial gives 6 AI generations (max 2 per day); PPTX or PDF export requires a Plus or Pro subscription. Paid tiers start around $9–12/month with annual discounts; lifetime licenses are available. Best for professionals and educators who want notes or existing content turned into slides quickly, with a familiar editor and drag-and-drop fine-tuning.

## What AiPPT does best

### AI generation and Nano Banana

Full decks from a prompt, document, or URL; AI can rewrite slide text, translate, rephrase bullets, and generate charts. Pro plans get more image credits and faster generation. The **Nano Banana 2** update (2026) improves image fidelity and detail for AI-generated visuals.

### Templates and design

Over 200,000 templates for business, education, and marketing; AI can suggest or swap designs. Custom fonts, colors, and logos with brand sync; drag-and-drop editing.

### Export and integration

PPTX, PDF, and PNG/JPG export; layouts preserve in PowerPoint and Google Slides. No public API for automation as of 2026.

## Verdict

AiPPT is a solid choice when you need a presentable deck in minutes from a topic or document. Strengths: speed, a very large template library, and flexible input sources. Quality can vary—some outputs need editing—and the free tier is limited (no export without paying). Best for founders, educators, and marketers who want fast first drafts and are okay subscribing for full export and daily limits. For highly bespoke designs or heavy multimedia, consider a traditional tool or another AI option.
