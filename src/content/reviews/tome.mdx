## Overview

**Tome Slides is no longer available.** Tome launched in 2022 as an AI-driven “narrative-first” presentation builder and grew to tens of millions of users, but in early 2025 the company announced it would shut down the Slides product and pivot. **All Tome Slides functionality was disabled on April 30, 2025.** The original team rebranded as **Lightfield**, an AI-powered sales CRM. The **Tome** name was acquired by AngelList for an AI document-summarization product—not presentations. This page is kept as historical and comparison context only.

## What Tome Slides was (2022–2025)

Tome generated slide decks from text prompts and supported rich embeds: Figma prototypes, Airtable/Sheets, YouTube, 3D (Spline), and more. It used GPT-based models for text and DALL·E for images; you could import PDFs or Word docs and get auto-generated slide blocks. The format was fluid and scrollable rather than classic 16:9 slides. Pricing was freemium (Basic free, Pro ~$16/mo); export was PDF and PNG only—no native PowerPoint export. Real-time collaboration and comments were strengths; limitations included limited layout control, no in-app fullscreen slideshow, and reliance on share links for viewing. When the product closed, user content was removed from the platform.

## Why it was discontinued

Public statements indicated the company could not make the Slides product sustainable despite large user growth. The team chose to focus on a narrower use case (sales intelligence and CRM with Lightfield) rather than continue the general-purpose presentation tool.

## Verdict (historical)

Tome was notable for **speed and narrative-style output** with **strong embed support**. It was a good fit for rapid storyboards and pitches when minimal editing and PDF delivery were acceptable. It was not a fit for teams that needed strict branding, native PPTX, or long-term reliance on a single vendor—as the shutdown showed. For similar “idea to deck fast” workflows today, consider **Gamma**, **Canva Magic Design**, **Beautiful.ai**, or **AiPPT**.
