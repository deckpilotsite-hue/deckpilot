## Overview

Beautiful.ai (Beautiful Slides, Inc.) is a San Francisco–based presentation platform built around design automation. Its core idea is **Smart Slides**: layouts that automatically adjust, resize, and align as you add or edit content, so you focus on the story instead of pixel-pushing. The product targets professional business presentations with strong brand controls, shared workspaces, and built-in viewer analytics. In 2026 the platform offers full-deck AI generation from prompts, AI text and image tools, and an early-access prompt-to-deck API. Pricing centers on Pro ($12/mo annually), Team ($40–50/user/mo), and Enterprise; a 14-day trial requires a credit card. Security and compliance (SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, CCPA, AES-256, HTTPS) and clear AI data policies (no training on customer data, optional enterprise opt-out) make it a plausible fit for regulated or brand-sensitive teams.

## Smart Slides and design automation

Smart Slides apply layout and typography rules automatically: add a bullet or change text and the slide adapts. The platform offers hundreds of Smart Slide layouts and supports tables, charts, timelines, org charts, and word clouds. Themes and templates keep decks visually consistent. The editor has been updated for a faster, cleaner workflow. Collaboration includes live editing, comments, and version history (Pro: per-slide revert, recover deleted slides). Export options include editable PPTX, PDF, and Google Slides; PowerPoint add-in lets you use Smart Slide technology inside PowerPoint.

## AI and integrations

Generative AI uses multiple models (e.g. Gemini and Claude for text, Imagen and DALL·E for images). AI presentation generation is step-by-step and outline-oriented rather than a single prompt-to-finished-deck. Integrations include Slack, Salesforce, Monday.com, ChatGPT (custom GPT), PowerPoint add-in, and Webex. The Beautiful.ai API (early access) can generate full, editable presentations via a POST request with Bearer token authentication.

## Verdict

Beautiful.ai is strongest when you need **fast, board- or client-ready decks** with **consistent branding** and **share links plus engagement analytics**. It’s a good fit for sales, marketing, and internal reporting where throughput and brand governance matter. It’s weaker when you need highly custom, pixel-level layout or when your workflow is strictly PowerPoint-centric and import/export fidelity is critical. User ratings split by channel: high on product-focused sites (e.g. G2 4.7), lower where billing and support dominate (e.g. Trustpilot). Trial the product and test export to your preferred format before committing.
